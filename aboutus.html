Mondo's Modules

Mondo's Modules is an experienced Eurorack modular synthesizer parts retailer in Karamalmi, Espoo. We offer the most diverse supply in Northern Europe with competitive prices.

Our company's idol, Mondo Maverick, was a Finnish electornic music pioneer who achieved global fame in the 80'. Mondo never took the easy way out in anything he did in his life, rather he lived completely according to his own values.

Mondo's Modules is a family business established in 2020. Our values and outlook on life are based on our respectable father Mondo Maverick.
